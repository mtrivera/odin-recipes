Enchiladas

Prep Time: 1 hour

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12-14 corn tortillas
  • 40 oz La Plumas Mild Red Enchilada sauce (or your favorite enchilada sauce)
  • 8 oz shredded cheese
  • 1 lb shredded chicken

Directions

  1. Get a glass 9x13 baking pan and coat lightly with non-stick spray.
  2. Prepare corn tortillas by lightly spreading oil on both side and heat 90 seconds each side. This makes them pialble and not fall apart during cooking. Set aside them aside.
  3. Shred 8 oz of cheese and store in refrigeator until ready. You can use less cheese if desired.
  4. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  5. Pour can of enchilada sauce into a steel pan and mix in shredded chicken.
  6. Dip corn tortillas into sauce, add chicken and cheese, roll into a taquito, and place into pan.
  7. Repeat until pan is full.
  8. Pour the extra sauce on the enchiladas to keep them moist.
  9. Sprinkle cheese onto them. Control how much cheese you want.
  10. Cover the pan with aluminum foil and place in center.
  11. Bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove from oven and remove foil.
  13. Let it sit for five minutes and enjoy with beans and rice if desired.
