Napoles and Eggs

Ingredients

  • 14 oz cactus
  • 3-4 roma tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 sweet onion (peeled and diced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (peeled and diced)
  • 2 large eggs

Directions

  1. Prepare napoles by removing pricklies and root. Cut into strips, bring to boil and simmer for 15 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Saute the onions for 3-5 minutes until translucent. Add garlic and cook for 90 seconds.
  3. Add tomatoes and salt and pepper for taste. Once liquid evaporates, add napoles
  4. Prepare the eggs in a seperate bowl using a whisk or fork.
  5. Cook for 5-7 napoles and add eggs to mix.
  6. Once eggs are cooked, turn off heat.
  7. Serve with beans, corn tortillas, tostadas or by iteself.
