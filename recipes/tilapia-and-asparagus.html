Tilapia and Aspargus

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 asparagus stalks
  • 2 tiliapia filets (4 oz each)
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Dry parsely

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Rinse asparagus and dry. Coat lightly with oil, salt, and pepper. Set aside
  3. Lightly brush tiliapia with oil and sprinkle them lemon juice
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley
  5. Dice the garlic clove and sprinkle the fish
  6. Put the fish and asparagus in the oven for 12 minutes
  7. Serve with quinoa or other grain and enjoy!
